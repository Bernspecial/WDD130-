About Us

Pelda Music was founded 2005 in Ibadan Oyo State, Nigeria. The band was founded by Mr Yemi Iyanda who is the CEO of the company also the band leader. The band consists of 5 vocalists, 3 guitarists, 2 keyboardist, 3 percussionists, and 2 drummers with a manager (whose job is to make sure the band gets all they need in every show). All our band members are professionally trained. Pelda music has travelled almost all the state in Nigeria, from the capital itself down to Lagos, Osun, and Kano just to mention few. Our music style is more of repertoire (which means the combinations of all the music genre, name it, hip-hop, reggae, afro beats etc.). We're one of the most organized musical band in Nigeria, no doubt about that, Our services are unique because we make use of all social platform to render our services, social platforms like: Twitter(which is now X), Facebook, Tik-tok, Zoom, Snapchat, Instagram.
